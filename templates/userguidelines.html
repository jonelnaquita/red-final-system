University Website Chatbot User Guidelines

Welcome to our University's website chatbot! This intelligent virtual assistant is designed to enhance your online experience by providing quick and helpful information. To ensure a smooth interaction, please familiarize yourself with the following guidelines:

Components

Our chatbot is equipped with various components to assist you effectively.

Prompts

Feel free to ask about admission, services, and other general information. Use clear and concise questions for the best results

Rating & Feedback

Share specific comments or suggestions to help us understand your experience better.

Chatbot Components

Chat Widget

The chat widget, conveniently located on the website, will display prior suggestions related to the topics you might be interested in.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Before using the chatbot, please review our Privacy Policy to understand how your data is collected, used, and protected. Your interactions with the chatbot are governed by our commitment to safeguarding your information.

Header

At the top of the chat interface, you'll find the Chatbot Header with the following options:

  • Fullscreen: Expand the chat window to fullscreen for a more immersive experience.
  • Text-to-Speech: Enable or disable the text-to-speech feature for auditory assistance.
  • Privacy Policy: Access our Privacy Policy to understand how your data is handled and protected.
  • Clear History: Clear your chat history for privacy or a fresh start.

Greetings

The chatbot will greet you and initiate the conversation. It will suggest frequently asked questions with predefined responses for quick assistance.

Input Message Options

  • Typing: You can input your messages by typing in the chatbox. The chatbot will respond accordingly to your queries.
  • Speech-to-Text: Alternatively, you can use the speech-to-text feature. Click the microphone icon in the chatbox, speak your question, and the chatbot will generate responses accordingly.
Prompts

Simple Questions

The chatbot excels at answering straightforward questions.

  • Good: "What are the admission requirements in the Batangas State University?"
  • Not ideal: "Can you tell me about the admission?"

Multiple Questions

You can ask the chatbot multiple questions in a single interaction. It's designed to handle conversations and maintain context throughout the chat. However, if you have complex inquiries, consider breaking them down for better clarity.

  • Good: "What are the admission requirements in the Batangas State University? Also, tell me about available scholarships."
  • Not ideal: "Tell me everything about the university."
Rate Conversation

Rating and Feedback

We value your feedback! After each interaction, you have the option to provide a rating and share your thoughts on the chatbot's performance. Your feedback helps us enhance the chatbot's capabilities and improve user satisfaction. If you are satisfied, you can simply express your gratitude by saying "thanks" or "thank you," and the rating and feedback options will appear.
